The City School develops and
strengthens youth to become
effective leaders for social justice.

We work with young people ages 14-19 living in Boston
and its surrounding suburbs who are emerging leaders
and have a passion for social justice. Our community
intentionally crosses lines of race, class, gender, and
neighborhood.

Our programs

We run two major programs, the Summer Leadership Program (SLP) and a school year program entitled Pathways to Change (PTC). We also offer social justice training and workshops through Social Justice Education Institute and Social Justice Leadership Institute.
